Reflections

The images on this page are reflections of events in Pastor Brown's life, also of family and duty to his faith, to which the Pastor held in the highest regard. It would be impossible to mark every milestone or special occasion that occurred during his 71 years of existence, or to show every person whose life was touched by him, and vice-versa. Hopefully, everyone who views this gallery of pictures will be touched in a positive way that allows one to keep Pastor Brown alive through memory, in the absence of his physical presence.
